Privacy Policy for Cat Pexeso

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Cat Pexeso handles information.

1. Information Collection and Use

Cat Pexeso does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data or user information. The game operates entirely locally on your device.

2. Game State and High Scores

Any game progress, high scores, or settings are saved locally on your device using the browser's local storage (LocalStorage). This data remains exclusively on your device and is never sent to any external servers or third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or cloud services. Your gameplay is private and offline-first.

4. Children's Privacy

Since the game does not collect any personal information, it is safe for players of all ages, including children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Microsoft Store support options.
